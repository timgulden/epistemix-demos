Stop loss risk assessment
Direct stop-loss carriers · executive-level
Forward-projected stop-loss exposure on a single submitted case, with scenario modeling for network change, coverage changes (GLP-1), and market changes (gene therapy). Built as a single-file HTML demo.

Tonal adoption
Tonal · investor-facing · macro curve + state machine
Interactive S-curve visualization of Tonal hardware and subscription adoption, with the original investor-deck projection rendered against the modeled trajectory and the gap between them shaded. Sliders and scenario presets drive the levers. A top-right toggle reveals the agent-level state machine — the per-individual mechanism beneath the macro curve.

Wellbeing strategy advisor
Benefits brokers · practice-leader-facing · strategy modeling
A broker-facing concept for modeling the most impactful employer wellbeing strategy. Three peelable layers — a combined strategy view, a live response model, and the mechanism beneath — let a benefits practice leader explore how interventions move population-level outcomes. Built as a single-file front-end mock for the Brown & Brown and Alliant conversations.
